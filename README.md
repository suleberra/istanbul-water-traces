# The Underlying Waters of Istanbul
### Exploring the spatial memory of water through street names, topography and urban infrastructure

This ongoing project investigates how Istanbul's historical relationship with water continues to shape the contemporary city. By combining geospatial analysis, historical research, and urban morphology, the project aims to uncover spatial patterns that reveal the hidden influence of water on today's urban landscape.

## Research Hypothesis

Street names containing water-related terms are expected to cluster around historical waterways and water infrastructure, reflecting Istanbul’s historical hydrological structure.

## Research Question(s)

How do historical water systems persist in the modern city through street names and spatial patterns?
